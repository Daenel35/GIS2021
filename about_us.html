KNOW OUR VALUES AND TEAM MEMBERS

Politecnico di Milano Master in Geoinformatics Engineering Students

These are the values that govern our teamwork

VALUE 1: Simplicity and Condensation

The simpler, the better. The more direct and summarized, the greater the impact. Modern world requires the use of bullet points to present any work done.

VALUE 2: Iteration of Cross-Validation

Write a paragraph, read it once and you might spot a couple of improvements, but make your peers read it and your quality will increase exponentially with each iteration. We split the work, but we check on each other's back.

VALUE 3: Always try the extra Mile

Following and completing the tutorials is always comforting, but just like in videogames we like to make good use of the skills learnt to roam free and explore other possible approaches and outcomes, going for the extra 1,60934 Km in logical units.
